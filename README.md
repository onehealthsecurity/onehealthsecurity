# One Health Security

Biosecurity | One Health | Epidemiological Modelling

---

## Overview

One Health Security focuses on the analysis and modelling of biological risk across animal, human, and environmental systems.

The work integrates policy modelling, outbreak simulation, and biological system design to support structured decision-making under uncertainty.

---

## Core Areas

### One Health Policy & Risk

Structured modelling of zoonotic risk and intervention strategies

### Outbreak Simulation

Stochastic modelling of infectious disease dynamics under uncertainty
